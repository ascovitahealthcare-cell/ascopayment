Accessibility Statement

Ascovita Healthcare — Committed to inclusive digital access

Ascovita Healthcare is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to people of all abilities, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

🎨
Visual Accessibility

High contrast colour ratios throughout the site. Text sizes are scalable and all images include descriptive alt text. We avoid relying solely on colour to convey information.

⌨️
Keyboard Navigation

All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard. Tab order follows a logical sequence and focus indicators are visible throughout the site.

📱
Mobile & Touch Accessible

Our site is fully responsive and designed for touch interfaces. Tap targets are adequately sized to ensure ease of use on mobile devices.

🔊
Screen Reader Support

Semantic HTML and ARIA labels are used throughout to support screen readers such as NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver on mobile devices.

🌐
Language & Readability

The site language is declared as English. Content is written in plain language. Line spacing and paragraph widths are optimised for readability.

🎬
Motion & Animation

Animations are kept subtle and non-essential. We respect the user's operating system preference for reduced motion via the prefers-reduced-motion CSS media query.

Known Limitations

We are continuously working to improve accessibility across all pages. Some third-party payment gateway elements (Cashfree) may not be fully under our control. We are working with our payment provider to ensure maximum accessibility compliance.

Subscription FAQs

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel, pause, or modify your subscription at any time from your Account Dashboard with zero penalties. Cancellations take effect before the next scheduled delivery.

How do subscription payments work?

Payment is charged automatically via your saved payment method (card/UPI) before each scheduled delivery. You'll receive a reminder email 3 days before each charge.

Can I change my product between deliveries?

Yes. Log in to your account dashboard and update your subscription product selection at least 5 days before the next scheduled delivery date.
